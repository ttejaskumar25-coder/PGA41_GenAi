# Inference, Serving & Deployment

Summary:
Focuses on serving models in production: latency/throughput trade-offs, batching, quantization, caching, and scalable deployment patterns.

Topics:
- Serving frameworks: FastAPI, Triton, TorchServe, Ray Serve
- Optimization: quantization, pruning, distillation, ONNX
- Infrastructure: containers, Kubernetes, autoscaling, GPUs/accelerators
- Monitoring: latency, accuracy drift, logging, alerting
