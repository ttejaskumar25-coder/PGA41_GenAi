# Introduction — What is an AI Engineer

Summary:
An AI Engineer designs, builds, and integrates AI systems into products. Responsibilities span data preprocessing, model training/fine-tuning, inference, evaluation, deployment, and monitoring. AI Engineers collaborate with ML researchers, data engineers, and product teams.

Key focus areas:
- Understanding LLMs, ML workflows, and production constraints
- Building reliable inference systems and pipelines
- Applying prompt engineering and retrieval techniques
- Ensuring safety, fairness, and robustness in deployed systems
