# Training & Fine-tuning

Summary:
Covers methods to adapt pre-trained models to specific tasks: fine-tuning, parameter-efficient tuning (LoRA, adapters), and supervised/unsupervised approaches.

Topics:
- Full fine-tuning vs parameter-efficient methods (LoRA, adapters)
- Dataset preparation and labeling best practices
- Evaluation metrics, validation, and overfitting detection
- Experiment tracking and reproducibility (Weights & Biases, MLflow)

Hands-on:
- Fine-tune a small transformer on a domain-specific dataset using Hugging Face Transformers
- Compare full fine-tuning vs LoRA for cost/quality trade-offs
